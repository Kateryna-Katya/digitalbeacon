Space Monster

Got a hungry black hole?
Time to feed it everything.

Control a hungry black hole through endless space — consume meteorites, grow massive, and keep Earth safe from the chaos.

How It Works

    • Steer the Void
    • Drag your black hole through space, lining up meteorites and cosmic debris in your path.
    • Consume & Grow
    • Every object you swallow makes your black hole bigger — and unlocks even larger targets to devour.
    • Protect Earth
    • The universe gets messier as you grow. Keep the debris away from Earth or the game is over.

Features

  • Endless Cosmic Chaos

    Each time you play, there's new stuff to eat, so no two games play the same.

  • One-Finger Control

    Super easy to start playing, but good luck stopping.

  • Size Matters

    Your black hole scales visibly as you eat — small pebbles early, then something much larger.

  • Free to Play

    No paywalls, no forced waits — just straight-up addictive gameplay.

About

Space Monster is a free mini IO game by fonfon, built for quick sessions and you'll get weirdly competitive about your high score. It takes the familiar "grow by consuming" loop and drops it into the gorgeous emptiness of space — you're basically a space vacuum cleaner, but cooler. Simple to start, hard to put down.

The universe won't clean itself.
