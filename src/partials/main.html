Space Monster

Got a hungry black hole?
Time to feed it everything.

Control a hungry black hole through endless space — consume meteorites, grow massive, and keep Earth safe from the chaos.

How It Works

    • Steer the Void
    • Drag your black hole through space, lining up meteorites and cosmic debris in your path.
    • Consume & Grow
    • Every object you swallow makes your black hole bigger — and unlocks even larger targets to devour.
    • Protect Earth
    • The universe gets messier as you grow. Keep the debris away from Earth or the game is over.
